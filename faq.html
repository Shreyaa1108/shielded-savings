Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Find quick solutions and detailed answers here.

What is a mortgage calculator?

+

A mortgage calculator helps you estimate your monthly payments based on loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.

How do I open a savings account?

+

You can open a savings account online or by visiting one of our branches with a valid ID and proof of address.

What is Shielded Savings' interest rate?

+

Our interest rates vary based on the account type. Please visit our Services page for detailed information.

How can I contact customer support?

+

You can reach us through the Contact Us page, by email, or by calling our 24/7 customer service helpline.

What types of mortgage plans do you offer?

+

We offer various mortgage plans including fixed-rate mortgages, adjustable-rate mortgages (ARMs), and interest-only mortgages. Our team can help you choose the best plan for your needs.

How do I apply for a loan with Shielded Savings?

+

To apply for a loan, simply visit our loan application page, fill in the required details, and submit your application. Our team will guide you through the next steps.

What should I do if I forget my online banking password?

+

If you forget your online banking password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive a recovery email or SMS to reset your password securely.
